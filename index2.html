Secure. Contain. Protect.

Object Class - Scale to determine danger of anomalies

Neutralized - Anomaly is neutralized

Safe - Anomaly is easily containable and poses almost no danger to the world outside

Euclid - Anomoly is containable, but it is unknown whether danger is posed by anomaly

Keter - Anomaly is hard to contain, and may pose great danger

Thaumiel - Anomaly is near impossible to contain, or is used as containment method, and has the possibility to cause immense damage

Apollyon - Anomaly is virtually uncontainable. Can and will breach at any given moment, can cause extinction level catastrophes

There are always exceptions to these classifications, keep that in mind
